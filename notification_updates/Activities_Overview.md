# Activities Overview - SEAM 2026

**Theme:** Our Life is His Message: Learning from Swami, Living in the Society

## Event Details
* **Venue:** Grischalodge, Hauptstrasse 22, 7076 Parpan, Switzerland
* **Dates:** 1st - 4th May 2026 (4 days)
* **Accommodation:** Grischalodge (main venue) + Jenatsch Hotel (opposite, across the road)

## Main Activities

### Spiritual & Cultural Sessions
* **Vedam Chanting** - Daily morning practice and evening contemplation
* **Bhajan Sessions** - Group singing of devotional songs (6 major sessions throughout the event)
* **Aarati** - Traditional lamp-lighting ceremony and closing prayers
* **Hanuman Chalisa** - Nightly recitation of this sacred hymn
* **Guest Talks** - Special address by Smt Geetha MohanRam on Swami's teachings and their application in life
* **Musical Offerings** - Dedicated music performances as spiritual expression
* **Drama Presentations** - Theatrical enactment of spiritual and moral themes

### Interactive & Reflection Activities
* **Open Mic Sessions** - Participants share thoughts, experiences, and insights
* **Breakout Group Discussions** - Small group conversations on "Living Swami's Message in the Society"
* **Parenting Chat & Q&A** - Special interactive session with Geetha Mohan Ram on parenting
* **Children's Music Program** - Musical activities and learning for younger participants
* **Sai Student Talks** - Sharing from youth participants

### Outdoor & Recreation Activities
* **Outdoor Group Photos** - Beautiful photo sessions in Alpine setting
* **Excursion to Squirrel Park & Lake Heidsee** - Nature walk to scenic Alpine destinations
  - Visit to Heidesee (small Alpine lake)
  - Walk through Eichhörnchenwald (Squirrel Forest)
  - Group photo at the lake with mountain backdrop

### Practical & Social Activities
* **Arrival & Registration** - Check-in and room settling
* **Networking Sessions** - Free time for informal conversations and community building
* **Meals & Dining** - Shared dining experience with freshly cooked vegetarian Indian cuisine
* **Room Checkout & Closing Announcements** - Final day housekeeping and farewells

## Daily Schedule Highlights

**Day 1 (Friday):** Welcome and arrival, introduction to the event theme, evening bhajans and community building

**Day 2 (Saturday):** Morning bhajans, guest lecture, outdoor breakout sessions on practical application of teachings, children's activities, evening music and bhajans

**Day 3 (Sunday):** Morning session with children's music program and guest talk, outdoor excursion to nature, evening drama and cultural performances

**Day 4 (Monday):** Closing ceremony, final bhajans and prayers, felicitations, and departure

## Special Notes
* All major group gatherings in the bhajan hall suggest wearing white dress. If you do not have it, wear something close to white and decent.
* Emphasis on both individual reflection and group harmony
* Suitable for all ages with dedicated children's activities
