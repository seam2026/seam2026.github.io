### Travel and Transportation Details

**Event Location:**
Grischalodge, Parpan, Switzerland
**Address:** Hauptstrasse 22, 7076 Parpan, Switzerland

**Getting There:**

*   **Nearest Airport:** Zurich Airport (approx. 2 hours by public transport)
*   **Nearest Train Station:** Zurich Hauptbahnhof (approx. 1 hour 40 mins by car)
*   **Public Transport:** Regular train and bus services connect Zurich to Parpan. From Zurich Hauptbahnhof, take a train to Chur and then a bus to Parpan (~2 hours from Zurich Haputbanhof).

**Shuttle Service:**
We plan to arrange a shuttle service from Zurich Hauptbahnhof to the event venue. Detailed schedules for the shuttle buses will be shared here closer to the event date.

**Parking:**
Parking is available on site for those driving to the venue.
