**Event Location:**
Grischalodge, Parpan, Switzerland
**Address:** Hauptstrasse 22, 7076 Parpan, Switzerland

**Getting There:**

*   **Nearest Airport:** Zurich Airport (approx. 2 hours by public transport)
*   **Public Transport:**
    *   **Zurich to Chur:** It takes approximately **1.5 hours** by train.
    *   **Chur to Parpan:** From Chur, it takes about **25 minutes** by bus to reach Parpan.
    *   **Frequency:** There are trains and buses running every **30 minutes**.
    *   **Tickets:** You can check schedules and book your tickets on the [SBB Website](https://www.sbb.ch/en). Alternatively, you can use the **SBB Mobile** app (available on App Store and Google Play).

**Shuttle Service:**
We plan to arrange cars from **Chur** to the event venue to supplement the public transport buses. Detailed schedules for this service will be shared here closer to the event date.

**Car Rental:**
For those preferring to drive, car rentals are available at Zurich Airport. You can book through major providers like **Sixt**, **Europcar**, **Hertz**, or **Avis**. Alternatively, you can compare prices and book via platforms like [Rentalcars.com](https://www.rentalcars.com) or [Kayak](https://www.kayak.com/cars). The drive to Parpan takes approximately 1 hour and 40 minutes.

**Parking:**
Free parking is available at the designated **Spielplatz (sports field)** near the venue. No payment is required.
* **Parking Details:** Up to 50 cars can be accommodated
* **Location Map:** [Spielplatz opposite the accommodation](https://maps.app.goo.gl/drMwZxAoLA1M7x3J9)
